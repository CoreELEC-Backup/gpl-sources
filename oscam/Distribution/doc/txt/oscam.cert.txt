NAME
       oscam.cert - Issuer Public Keys (IPK) for OSCam

SYNOPSIS
       Issuer Public Keys (IPK)

DESCRIPTIONS
       CAID:reserved:IPK
	  mapping between CAID and IPK/sessions keys in hex, currently for Cryptoworks only

EXAMPLES
	0100:00000000:0102030405060708090A0B0C0D0E0F
